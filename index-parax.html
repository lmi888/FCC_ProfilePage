About

It all started with a search for cat-safe plants. When I have enough knowledge, I plan on creating a website that will list all cat-safe houseplants, be searchable, and have large photos which will make identification of plants easy when out and about plant shopping. This means a well-written and designed mobile site, as well as desktop. Care instructions will be included too! I have big ideas for this project and I know it will be something to be proud of once it's completed.

Once upon a time, somewhere around 14 years ago, I was a contractor for a large computer company. There I did some sysadmin and hardware work, but I had the most fun doing HTML, XML and XSLT in their webmasters department. I stopped doing that work for a multitude of reasons, and as you can probably figure out, my knowledge is very outdated. The web and it's technologies have come a long way! Therefore I'm currently taking Colt Steele's Web Development class at Udemy and the Web Developer class at FreeCodeCamp. I have always been interested in, and am still having fun with HTML and CSS! I plan to study Python in depth, and will be taking Javascript classes as well.

I have a multitude of creative outlets, from mosaic to origami to cross-stitch! The one I've stuck to the most though, has been crochet. I was taught how to crochet by my grandmother when I was a little girl, and I've been at it ever since.

I am owned by two beautiful cats, King Tut and Loki. Here's their board on Pinterest.
